Tagliolini Cacio e Pepe

A classic Italian spaghetti dish

Creamy white sauce made with pecorino romano and freshly cracked black pepper

Ingredients

  • kosher salt
  • six ounces of tagliolini (spaghetti)
  • three tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • one teaspoon of freshly cracked black pepper
  • one cup of finely grated parmesan
  • one half cup of finely grated pecorino

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil. Season with salt; add past and cook, stirring occasionally. Drain, reserving some pasta water.
  2. Melt butter in large pot over medium heat. Add pepper, swirl pan and cook for two minutes.
  3. Add some pasta warer to skillet and bring to simmer. Add pasta and butter. Add cheese, stirring and tossing until melted.
